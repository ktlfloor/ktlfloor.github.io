Our wood sourcing

Our wood sourcing

From ancient time, wood have been one of the natural resources components used for building material construction. It was used as it is renewable source from the natural resources. Therefore, KTL Floors naturally choose these forest natural resources for its flooring with great emphasis that only wood resources from sustainable forest managed and controlled sources material are used in the production of its flooring.

 

It was our Company policy to support in reserving these precious natural resources from the forest for future generations and our production factory in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, have been certified under the Programme for the Endorsement of Forest Certification schemes (PEFC) to ensure that all its products is from sustainable managed forest and controlled sources. With the PEFC label we can ensure that we can give our customers and end users the confident that all raw material used in our products is from raw material originated from sustainable managed forests.
