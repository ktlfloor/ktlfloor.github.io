White Oak 2-strip Premium matt lacquer

A light brownish, uniform and harmonious look with minor colour- and structure variation between the staves, including minor sound and black knots.

Product Categories : Solid parquet flooring boards, White Oak

Product: Solid White Oak 2-strip Premium White matt lacquer
 
Specie: White Oak. Botanical name: Quercus robur L. inkl. Q. pertraea og Quercus spp. inkl. Q. alba m.fl. (N.America / EU)
 
Dimension: 22x130mm Length: 2.180mm.
 
Packaging: Supplied in bundles with 6 boards per bundle, of which one length may consist of two half lengths.
 
Board profile: The parquet board has tongue/groove along all 4 sides, and with sharp edges. Stave ends are finger jointed into strips, which are assembled longitudianally by means of glue.
 
Surface: 7-layer UV lacquer system incl. 2 layer anti scratch topcoat. Gloss: Matt, 15-20
 
Producered and CE marked acc. to EN 14342:2005 + EN 13629.
