About KTL Sports Flooring

"HIGH QUALITY SPORTS FLOORING BRINGS THE BEST OUT OF EVERY ATHLETE"

 

At KTL SPORTS FLOORING, this understanding has been underlying philosophy in developing world class sport flooring systems 

that exceed the expectations of even the most demanding athletes in today's highly competitive sports community. Athelets should

be able to trust and rely on KTL SPORTS FLOORING to be safe and provide full support, sability and resilience, while they can focus

and channel their efforts on winning the game.

 
 

THE BEST OF INGREDIENTS

KTL SPORTS FLOORING are a result of unique creation technique integrated with the best of ingredients.
 

  • Unique combination of Malaysian craftsmanship and Danish expertise.

  • More than 30 years of experience in creating the best of solid hardwood flooring for customers across the world.

  • Creation of high quality sports flooring solutions using key technical know-how from Danish Engineer and Danish Designer with more than 20 years of experience in sport floor systems.

  • Superior sport floor systems that meet most stringent demands from athletes on performance and safety, designed with standards of efficiency and security for the installers and comes as durable, maintenance-friendly and long-lasting solutions for the benefit of the owners.

  • Comprehensive technical support with full documentation and personalised service right from design phase, including specifications, laying instructions and maintenance guides complete our unique package.



 		      
     
     
     
     

 

QUALITY ACCREDITED

KTL SPORTS FLOORING 

   

   

  • Fully comply with the most stringent international standard EN 14904:2006.

  • Tested by the Technological Institute of Denmark.

  • Are marked CE in accordance with the EN 14342:2005+A1:2008 and EN 13629 standards
