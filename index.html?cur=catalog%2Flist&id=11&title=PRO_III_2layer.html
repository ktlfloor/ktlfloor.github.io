PRO III 2layer

  • Double batten system with high shock absorbency and resilience.

  • Comply with EN 14904 - Class A4 standard.

  • Installed as an upper batten in a 2 layer system, this PRO III 2layer solution is ideal in renovations, where greater construction heights are required.

  • Using our 40x45mm raiser batten in combination with the unique wedge sytem, it covers heights from 150 to 210mm.

  • Build-in deformation control of elastic shock pads and heavy duty loading capacity.

  • Choice of 2 batten distances depending on the floor, wood species and the loading capacity requirements.

  • Fast and easy to install.

PRO III 2layer

PRO III 2layer is a high performance double batten system, installed over uneven concrete using different leveling material depending on required construction height. Construction height including 22mm parquet flooring board, without additional packing, is 148mm. Comply with EN 14904, class A4 standard.

Components:
- 22x130mm solid hardwood flooring boards
- Machine nails
- PRO III battens
- Lower batten
- Packing
- Moisture barrier
- Concrete
