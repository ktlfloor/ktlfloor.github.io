Cleaning & Maintenance

FLOORS FOR LIFE

  
Game lines and maintenance lacquer
 		  
KTL SPORTS FLOORING are prefinished with a hardwea-ring multi coating UV hardened lacquer system that complies with EN 14904. Floors are ready for use immediately after installation.

No extra lacquer is required after installation, as this may only reduce the performance of the floor.
 
Line marking carried out using special 2-pack solvent based paint can be applied directly onto the factory finish without applying a protective coat of lacquer afterwards. We recommend WearMax floor paint.
 
Alternatively, a Water based 2-component line marking system can be applied, however, here a top coat of maintenance lacquer is required to protect line markings. 
We recommend WearMax EkoLac 2220 linemarking system. 
And for the first coat of maintenance lacquer, we always recommend WearMax EkoLac 8000 sports lacquer.
 
Before applying a maintenance coat, the floor surface must be clean, dry and free from greases etc. Buff the floor using min. grid #120. Vacuum to remove all dust, and then apply the maintenance coat.
 
Study our Maintenance guide carefully before start! 

 
Daily maintenance of the floor
 
Daily maintenance is normally limited to
sweeping to remove dust and to maintain the
friction of the floor. KTL SPORTS FLOORING
boards have high resistance against water,
therefore, allowing frequent cleaning of the
floor. Cleaning is best done with scrubber–
dryer machines.
 
Surface renovations
 
The floor surface should be renovated before a wear through of the lacquer system. The lifetime of a lacquer system is very individual as it purely depends on the usage of the floor. The existing maintenance lacquer on the floor is buffed before applying a new coat of lacquer. As the flooring boards are 22mm thick and of solid hardwood, they can be sanded to bare wood and re-coated several times, ensuring extended lifetime with KTL SPORTS FLOORING solutions.

Long term performance and durability
 
All components used in KTL SPORTS FLOORING systems undergo stringent test procedures. This delivers long term
performance of the entire flooring constructions. With proper maintenance KTL SPORTS FLOORING solutions are expected to last over 30 years. A great value for your
investment and optimising your Total Cost of Ownership.
