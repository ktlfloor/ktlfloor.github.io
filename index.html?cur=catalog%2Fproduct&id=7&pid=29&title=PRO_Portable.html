PRO Portable

PRO Portable System is designed for ultimate performance in high level sports, made from 22mm solid hardwood panels on plywood and foam underlay, in a construction height of 44mm. Comply with EN 14904, class A4 standard.

Components:
- 22x550x2180mm solid hardwood flooring panels (For wood species, see under images)
- Flexy-lock clip system
- 12mm birch plywood strips (battens + along all edges)
- 10mm special foam
- Subfloor: Existing sports floor or concrete leveled to +/- 2mm over a 2m straight edge.

Product Categories : Portable floor systems, PRO Portable
